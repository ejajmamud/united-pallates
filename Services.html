The Benefits of

Pallet Recycling

Ensures Lower Costs

Recycled pallets can cost up to 30% less than new pallets. By recycling pallets, your business is doing its part to ensure that there are enough recycled pallets in circulation at all times.

Environmental Sustainability

Recycling pallets reduces new lumber usage, helps minimize emissions than new pallets.

Can Generate Revenue

Depending on the condition and size / style of the pallet, certain pallets can be sold to pallet companies like United Pallets.

Pallet recycling benefits your business and our planet.
