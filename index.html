About Our National
Pallet Recycling Company

United Pallets is a premier pallet manufacturer and recycler that provides local wood pallet services on a national scale across Malaysia.

Operating from our hub in Rawang, we service customers every day with the ability to pick up your pallets and allow pallet yard drop-offs. Our custom programs address your specific needs and are delivered by a local team dedicated to your success.

As a leading pallet provider, we have access to vast resources that allow us to respond to your demands quickly. We ensure timely deliveries and rely on industry-leading capabilities to add value to your program and ensure continued transparency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Recycled pallets are a cost-effective alternative to new timber. They significantly reduce carbon emissions by extending the lifecycle of existing wood products and save millions of trees from being cut down for new production annually.

Our pallet recycling program directly reduces deforestation, saves substantial CO2 emissions associated with manufacturing new pallets, and diverts usable wood waste away from rapidly filling landfills.
