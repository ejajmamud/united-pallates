Committed to Excellence

Established in 2019, United Pallets is currently operating in Rawang, Selangor. Our core business and expertise is manufacturing wooden pallets & boxes.

Our Expertise

Being able to have design, manufacturing and delivery under one roof gives our customers the assurance that their pallets and boxes needs are well taken care of. Also, by having complete ownership of our manufacturing facility we can guarantee confidence in product quality and continuity of supply.

We aspire to provide our customers with the utmost in service, excellence and productivity. Hence, we are committed on employee training so as to maintain the highest level of workmanship, at the same time, adhering to the regulations of domestic or foreign requirements.

Our Vision

Our vision, in relation to sustainability, is a world where supply chains produce zero wood waste, with minimum negative impact on our climate and eco-systems.

To achieve our sustainability vision, United Pallets has made significant investments at all stages of our “Sustainable Pallet Life-Cycle” to ensure we have the most comprehensive supply chain coverage for our customers and vendors. This ability allows us to continuously retain the maximum amount of pallets into inventory, while creating value out of “End of Life” pallets via environmentally positive byproducts such as Mulch, Biofuel, and Wood Pellets.

Sustainable Pallet Cycle

1. Build

We build wooden pallets from materials that promote healthy forest.

2. Repair

Our experts perform repairs to extend the life of a pallet.

3. Remanufacture

We dismantle odd-size pallets and harvest their components to build re-manufactured pallets.

4. Lifecycle End

Once a pallet reaches its end of life, our team will grind it into products such Mulch, Biofuel, and Pellets.
